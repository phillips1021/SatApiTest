Teacher and Learner

The teacher works with learners from diverse backgrounds and experiences in a non-judgemental manner across the spectrum of development. The teacher sets goals with learners and facilitates reflection to promote progress toward desired outcomes. The teacher collaborates with learners and the learners' institutions to address difficulties and identify areas for growth.
