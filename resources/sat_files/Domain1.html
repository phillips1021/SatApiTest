Teacher and Professionalism

The teacher role models professional behavior and fosters the development of learners' professional identities. The teacher effectively manages relationships with appropriate ethical boundaries, remaining mindful of bias and potential conflicts of interest. In all settings, the teacher promotes a culture of well-being and acceptance.
